How it Works

A seamless clinic
workflow, end-to-end

DentalOS mirrors the natural flow of your clinic — from patient arrival to payment and lab dispatch.

01

Patient Registration & Appointment

The receptionist registers the patient with full demographics, medical history, and contact details. Appointments are booked instantly and tracked in real-time by status: Pending → Confirmed → Completed.

Patient ModuleAppointment BookingStatus Tracking
02

Check-In & Today's Queue

When the patient arrives, they're added to "Today's Queue." The doctor can instantly view their full history, previous visits, and all uploaded X-rays and files. Smart notifications alert staff of new arrivals.

Daily QueuePatient HistoryFile UploadsNotifications
03

Consultation & e-Prescription

The doctor records the diagnosis, selects treatments from the catalog, and generates a digital e-prescription using medication templates — in seconds. Prescriptions are linked to the visit record permanently.

e-PrescriptionTreatment CatalogMedication Templates
04

Lab Work Order & Print

For prosthetics or lab work, the doctor creates a lab order — selecting the work type, lab partner, and due date. The system generates a printable lab slip with all details ready to hand to the lab.

Lab Work OrdersPrint WorkflowStatus Tracking
05

Billing & Payment

Treatments are automatically pulled into billing. The receptionist generates a professional A4 invoice, marks it as paid on collection, and prints or shares the receipt. Revenue is tracked instantly on the dashboard.

A4 InvoicePayment TrackingDashboard Analytics
06

Reports & CSV Export

Admins can view daily, monthly, and yearly analytics at a glance. Any module's data — patients, billing, appointments, inventory — can be exported to CSV with one click for use in Excel or accounting tools.
